About SalarySight

SalarySight is a tool designed to provide salary estimates in the United States based on job titles, company size, industry, and other factors. Our mission is to help job-seekers and employees make informed decisions about their career paths.

Developed by Adel Kayyali, a data scientist and engineer, SalarySight uses advanced algorithms and up-to-date market data to predict compensation ranges. Our goal is to bring transparency to the job market and empower individuals with the knowledge they need to negotiate salaries confidently.
